Enigma North focuses on the development and porting of Minecraft plugins, making high-quality gameplay expansions available on the latest Paper server versions.

We maintain unofficial forks of popular plugins — updated for current Minecraft releases — so server administrators can enjoy them without waiting for upstream support.

All plugins are open-source and available on [GitHub](https://github.com/Repulsiveprogress).
